Engineering and Robotics

Engineering and Robotics combine principles of mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering to design, build, and operate robots and automated systems. This multidisciplinary field empowers innovations in industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, and transportation, enabling machines to perform tasks autonomously or assist human workers in complex environments. Robotics is paving the way for smarter systems that integrate artificial intelligence, machine learning, and advanced control mechanisms for efficient, reliable automation.
