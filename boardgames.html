Board Games

Board games are a fun and interactive way to enhance learning, social skills, and critical thinking. At STEMSupply, we offer a selection of educational and recreational board games that make learning enjoyable. Whether it's strategy games for older students or simple counting games for younger children, our platform ensures access to quality games that foster collaboration, problem-solving, and creative play.

Othelo

$14.00

A game of strategy, Othello, often confused with Reversi, is played between two players on an 8x8 uncheckered board where each person tries to turn the board their color.

Pylos

35$

On a truncated board with 16 hollows and two sets of spheres (light and dark), players take turns building a pyramid by placing their pieces. The goal is to keep as many spheres as possible in the hope of placing the final piece on top.

Forbidden Island

$31.25

Those looking for an adrenaline rush without the actual danger aspect can embark on a do-or-die mission. Work with your team to ascertain four treasures before the island sinks—as it does with every step.

Blokus

$25.90

Those who love Tetris or have strong spatial awareness will enjoy this game. In the box are 84 geometrically-shaped pieces in four colors with 21 in each color (each player takes a color).

Catan

$62.50

Previously known as The Settlers of Catan, this game was first released in Germany by Franckh-Kosmos Verlag as Die Siedler von Catan in 1995. The multiplayer board game challenges players to—you guessed it—settle the uncharted island of Catan.

Monopoly

$54.00

Appealing to all ages, Monopoly keeps players occupied for a solid amount of time and requires the perfect amount of luck and brains to be successful. As such, it’s much like life.
