Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering

Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering involve the design, development, and testing of aircraft, spacecraft, and mechanical systems. These engineering fields require a deep understanding of physics, materials science, and fluid dynamics to create efficient and safe transportation systems for air and space travel. Aerospace engineers focus on designing systems that operate in the challenging conditions of the atmosphere and beyond, while mechanical engineers apply principles of mechanics and thermodynamics to develop and optimize everything from engines to robotics, ensuring reliability and performance.
